Hygiene and Sanitation Programme

This two fold programme addressing poor saniation practices in rural communities. Thousands of households and schools utilize long-drop toilets which are not properly maintained and waste is left untreated for a long periods. Through this programme a solution for proper maintance and treatment of these toilets is applied.


This is the educational wing of the programme, where trainings on the manufacturing of detergent is given through our rural innovation hub.

Life Preservation and Property Protection Programme

GJCBC has incorporated an existing indigenous protection structure within the community to protect all programmes and projects providing a safer protection.

Indiginization Programme

This programme reunites people with their roots and heritage. It holds emphasized on the respect of traditional leaderships, community-identity. Through this programme communities are able create their wealth based on their own indigenous standards and values. This includes heritage and cultrual workshops alongside many other activities to bring people closer to understanding where they come from and how to use that knowledge to get to where they want to go.
