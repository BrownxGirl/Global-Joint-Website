About

Global Joint Council Of Business Communities (GJCBC) is a registered South African Non-Profit Company(formly known as an NGO), which focuses on policy development and implementation that contributes in sustainable rural development and growth. We have been intimately involved in creating change in rural communities through our programmes and onsite rural development and growth research study.

Our Mission

Our mission is to assist grassroot businesses to grow and be sustainable through:

  • Lobbying for financial and technical support
  • Business linkages
  • Marketing and promotion of rural businesses

Our Foucus

We are focused on the below point in order to achieve a holistic impact within the community

  • Food Security
  • Indigenization
  • Human Capital Investment (Investing in the people's growth)
  • Protection of Property and Life Preservation
